Shooting

Target shooting and airsoft are two of my favourite passtimes where possible. I was in the ACF for 6 years, unfortunately the final two being during covid, which is where I discovered my love of shooting and firearms in general, it's also where I first met TDA's own Neil Bizzell.


I did actually start off with a very entry level air rifle before I joined the cadets, but found a lot more enjoyment firing live rounds and occasionally some very high end PCP rifles owned by a SSGT, my favourites of the many being the L98A2 and the AirArms S510 Carbine.


During my time away from instructing I took the time to become very familiar with the inner workings of the L98 and firearms in general, eventually learning to field strip and reassamble one blindfolded... once.

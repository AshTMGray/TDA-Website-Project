Music

Music has been a huge part of my life since I started secondary school. My first and since main instrument was the humble piano, I was self-taught for about 7 years before spending a year doing classical training and working towards my grade 5 ABRSM certificate.


My favourite classical piece currently is Chopin's Nocturne No.20 in C# Minor, a grade 7 ABRSM piece so a little above my station but I decided to learn and record it either way. Unfortunately I don't have a piano where I live at the moment, but I hope to update this with a more current recording.

There is a small mistake nearing the end, please excuse the colourful language!
